Our Founding story

Omanga Law Advocates was founded by ALEX OMANGA, an advocate of the high court of Kenya with deep expertise in litigation, constitutional law, and commercial legal matters. His vision was to establish a modern, ethical law firm that would combine legal excellence with a truly client-centered approach

To meet the growing demand for accessible and regionally grounded legal services, the firm expanded to include two strategically positioned offices - in Kisumu and Nairobi CBD - each led by highly experienced managing partners.

Today the firm operates under the leadership of:
